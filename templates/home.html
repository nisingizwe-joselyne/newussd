PopSchools is meant to create access to a safe and inclusive community, mutual aid, and paying work opportunities through
apprenticeships to early-career software developer

PopSchools is a collective community effort.
We are currently building the PopSchools community
primarily through our newsletter, GitHub,
and our community at popschools.club.
Our website is under construction
but you can expect to find out more about us,
what we do, and who has supported us there soon.

Meet our founder

My work is about creating opportunities for technologists like myself
who are just getting started in their tech careers.
I recognize from experience the extra effort it takes to get a great start
when you are dealing with financial hardships and a number of non-negotiable adult responsibilities.
When you sponsor me, you allow me the mental space to do my work to the best of my ability.
