SMART IKIGEGA SYSTEM

This is the system that helps farmers to be inclusive in many services digitally.with smart ikigega farmers are able to use cashless method and they are able to be identified with single identity (farmercode) which helps them to acces other neccessary services(bank &crop insurance) digitally beyond to that they are able to access and record their harvesting data digital using USSD Portal.

SMART TRANSPORT FOR ALL

with this system we are bringing the inclusivity of visual and hearing impaired people in general transport. we want to make sure that they are able to be independent in their journey while using general transport by getting sound and sign notification of every bus station that they are approaching this will helps them to feel comfortable while using general transport like bus in our country.
